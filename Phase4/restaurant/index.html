Located in East and West El Paso, we are a small local restaurant that serves simple, unpretentious, yet delicious food in a casual environment. The majority of our menu items are cooked in our very own, hand made wood-fired brick oven. Our fresh mozzarella is made in-house daily as well as our properly fermented dough for our rustic bread and pizza
